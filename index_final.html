Technical Specifications

System Architecture

A four-layer intelligent control stack: sensing, processing, inference, and actuation.

Edge Conditioning

High-fidelity data extraction from ADXL345 and DHT22 sensors via I2C and ESP32 Devkit V1 edge-processing layers. Tri-axis acceleration fused into resultant vibration intensity.

Pattern Learning

Moving beyond fixed thresholds: Python-based intelligence identifies fatigue patterns before physical failure occurs. Adaptive inference trained on labeled Normal / Warning / Critical states.

Physical Actuation

Closed-loop relay interlocks and BC547 transistor-driven cooling fans respond to system decisions in milliseconds. Warning activates cooling; Critical triggers full motor cutoff.
